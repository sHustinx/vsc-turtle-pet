# vsc-turtle-pet
## Your emotional support turtle for VSCode 🐢

Could you use a little emotional support while coding?

Meet *Mono the turtle*, your calm, low-maintenance companion who lives quietly in your sidebar. Like any proper turtle, he mostly does his own thing: occasionally moving around, minding his business, and reminding you to take things easy.

He also enjoys the occasional pet ❤️
